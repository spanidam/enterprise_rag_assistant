these to control the motion of the manipulator in real time.
Over the years, a variety of approaches have been taken to the problem
of vision-based control.
These vary based on how the image data is used,
the relative conﬁguration of camera and manipulator, choices of coordinate
systems, etc.
In this chapter, we focus primarily on one speciﬁc approach:
image-based visual servo control for eye-in-hand camera conﬁgurations.
