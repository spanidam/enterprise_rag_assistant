9.2 • Sorting and reversing lists
227
# Reverse the list
dance_list.reverse()
print(dance_list)
# Sort the list
dance_list.sort()
print(dance_list)
The above code's output is:
[38, 92, 23, 16]
[16, 23, 38, 92]
["Dabke", "Flamenco", "Hopak", "Kathak", "Salsa", "Ballet", "Stepping"]
["Ballet", "Dabke", "Flamenco", "Hopak", "Kathak", "Salsa", "Stepping"]
