4.2 A note on Adam
Adam [Kingma and Ba, 2015] is a variant of the Stochastic Gradient Descent method. It differs in the definition of the
steps to be performed at each parameter update.
First, it uses what is called momentum, which basically consists in relying on past gradient updates to smooth out the
trajectory in parameter space during optimization. An interactive illustration of momentum can be found in [Goh, 2017].
