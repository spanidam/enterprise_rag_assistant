ATTENTION MECHANISM
In many deep learning contexts (machine translation, text summarization, sequence processing), models must handle
variable-length inputs and focus on certain parts more than others.
The attention mechanism allows the model to give more weight to certain elements of a sequence when computing an
output, depending on their relevance.
