Remark 7.6.1
We note from (7.53) that the characteristic polynomial of the closed loop sys-
tem is identical to (7.33). The system now however is written in terms of the
tracking error e(t). Therefore, assuming that the closed loop system is stable,
the tracking error will approach zero asymptotically for any desired joint space
trajectory in the absence of disturbances, that is, if d = 0.
