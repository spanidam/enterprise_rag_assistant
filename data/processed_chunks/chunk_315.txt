What values should be chosen for a1 and a2?
If
we design the robot to maximize the maximum manipulability, the we need to
maximize µ = a1a2|s2|. We have already seen that the maximum is obtained
when θ2 = ±π/2, so we need only ﬁnd a1 and a2 to maximize the product a1a2.
This is achieved when a1 = a2. Thus, to maximize manipulability, the link
lengths should be chosen to be equal.
