If there are multiple convex obstacles Oi, then the conﬁguration space obsta-
cle region is merely the union of the obstacle regions QOi, for the individual
obstacles. For a nonconvex obstacle, the conﬁguration space obstacle region can
be computed by ﬁrst decomposing the nonconvex obstacle into convex pieces,
Oi, computing the C-space obstacle region, QOi for each piece, and ﬁnally,
computing the union of the QOi.
