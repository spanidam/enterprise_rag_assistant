LLMs can also be augmented by using external knowledge
and tools [35], [36] so that they can effectively interact with
users and environment [37], and continually improve itself
using feedback data collected through interactions (e.g. via
reinforcement learning with human feedback (RLHF)).
Through advanced usage and augmentation techniques,
LLMs can be deployed as so-called AI agents: artificial entities
that sense their environment, make decisions, and take actions.
