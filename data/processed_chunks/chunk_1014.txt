8.5 Self-attention
In some cases, such as sequence processing, the inputs 𝑋and 𝑌are the same sequence (we want to compare the elements
of the sequence pairwise): this is called self-attention.
This means that each position in the sequence 𝑋“looks at” all other positions in that same sequence to build its own
representation.
