9.4
TASK SPACE DYNAMICS AND CONTROL
Since a manipulator task speciﬁcation, such as grasping an object, or inserting
a peg into a hole, is typically given relative to the end- eﬀector frame, it is
natural to derive the control algorithm directly in the task space rather than
joint space coordinates.
