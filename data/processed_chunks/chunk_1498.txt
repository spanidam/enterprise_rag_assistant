Classes and instances
Consider the example below:
class Cat:
def __init__(self):
self.name = 'Kitty'
self.breed = 'domestic short hair'
self.age = 1
def print_info(self):
print(self.name, 'is a ', self.age, 'yr old', self.breed)
pet_1 = Cat()
pet_2 = Cat()
