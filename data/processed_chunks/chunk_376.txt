RPP (for Randomized Potential Planner). The basic approach is straightfor-
ward: use gradient descent until the planner ﬁnds itself stuck in a local mini-
mum, then use a random walk to escape the local minimum. The algorithm is
a slight modiﬁcation of the gradient descent algorithm of Section 5.3.
