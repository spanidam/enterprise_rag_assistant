With an eye-in-hand system, the camera is often attached to the manipu-
lator above the wrist, i.e., the motion of the wrist does not aﬀect the camera
motion. In this way, the camera can observe the motion of the end eﬀector
at a ﬁxed resolution and without occlusion as the manipulator moves through
the workspace. One diﬃculty that confronts the eye-in-hand conﬁguration is
