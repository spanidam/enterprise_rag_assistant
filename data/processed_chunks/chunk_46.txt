Cylindrical Manipulator (RPP)
The cylindrical manipulator is shown in Figure 1.16. The ﬁrst joint is revolute
and produces a rotation about the base, while the second and third joints
are prismatic.
As the name suggests, the joint variables are the cylindrical
coordinates of the end-eﬀector with respect to the base. A cylindrical robot, the
Seiko RT3300, is shown in Figure 1.17, with its workspace shown in Figure 1.18.
