Consider the two-link planar arm of Figure 3.6. The joint axes z0 and z1
are normal to the page. We establish the base frame o0x0y0z0 as shown. The
origin is chosen at the point of intersection of the z0 axis with the page and
the direction of the x0 axis is completely arbitrary.
Once the base frame is
established, the o1x1y1z1 frame is ﬁxed as shown by the DH convention, where
the origin o1 has been located at the intersection of z1 and the page. The ﬁnal
frame o2x2y2z2 is ﬁxed by choosing the origin o2 at the end of link 2 as shown.
