Tool frame assignment
In most contemporary robots the ﬁnal joint motion is a rotation of the end-
eﬀector by θn and the ﬁnal two joint axes, zn−1 and zn, coincide. In this case,
the transformation between the ﬁnal two coordinate frames is a translation
along zn−1 by a distance dn followed (or preceded) by a rotation of θn about
zn−1. This is an important observation that will simplify the computation of
the inverse kinematics in the next section.
